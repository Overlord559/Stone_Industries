Privacy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

This page describes how the Stone Industries marketing website handles information. The site is a brochure-style marketing presence. It does not include user accounts, online checkout, or booking calendars.

Information this site collects

The public website itself does not use analytics scripts, advertising pixels, or cookies for tracking as part of this baseline build.

Service inquiry forms

The homepage, pricing page, and services page include optional inquiry forms so you can request a quote or describe what you need. If you submit a form, Stone Industries may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address and/or phone number (at least one is required)
  • Service requested
  • City or service area (optional)
  • Your message
  • The page you submitted from (source page)
  • Submission date and time (stored automatically)

This information is used to respond to your inquiry, provide service estimates, and follow up about Stone Industries services. When configured, form submissions are stored in a Supabase database operated for Stone Industries business use. Stone Industries does not sell this information.

You can also contact Stone Industries by email or phone without using the form. Email and phone links on the site remain available as a fallback if the online form is unavailable or you prefer direct contact.

Please do not submit passwords, payment card numbers, bank account details, Social Security numbers, medical records, or other highly sensitive personal information through the inquiry form.

Data retention

Inquiry records are kept for business follow-up and service quoting. Stone Industries does not sell inquiry data. If you want an inquiry removed after submitting, contact stoneindustries0.llc@gmail.com with enough detail to identify the submission (name, date, and contact method used).

Direct email and phone contact

If you contact Stone Industries directly by email or phone using the details shown on the site, that communication happens outside the inquiry form. Any information you choose to share in direct contact is handled for business communication purposes.

Hosting and technical logs

The site is hosted on Netlify (production) with GitHub Pages as a mirror. Hosting providers may process standard server and delivery logs as part of providing hosting service. Refer to your host's documentation for platform practices.

Third-party links

The site may link to external services such as email or phone clients. Those services are governed by their own policies once you leave this site.

Contact

Questions about this page can be sent to stoneindustries0.llc@gmail.com.

This page is provided for basic business-site transparency. It is not legal advice and has not been reviewed by counsel.
