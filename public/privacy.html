Stone Industries LLC ("Stone Industries," "we," "us") operates the Stone Industries marketing website and related business communications. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit the site, submit forms, email us, call us, or schedule a consultation.

Who we are

Stone Industries LLC is a California limited liability company providing technology services, AI customer systems, and GovCon support services. Questions about this policy may be sent to edward@stoneindustriesusa.com.

Information we collect

Depending on how you contact us, we may collect:

  • Inquiry and audit forms: name, email, phone, service requested, city or service area, message content, and the page you submitted from.

  • Scheduling: information you provide through Calendly or similar booking tools linked from our site.

  • Technical logs: hosting providers may process standard server and delivery logs.

How we use information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to inquiries, quotes, audits, and service requests

  • Send invoices, payment links, and project updates
  • Maintain business records and improve our services

We do not sell personal information.

Service providers

We may use third-party tools to operate the business, including email and workspace tools (such as Google Workspace), scheduling (Calendly), customer relationship tools (HubSpot), payment processors (Stripe, Bluevine), accounting (Wave), hosting and security (Cloudflare, Netlify), database services (Supabase), and analytics where enabled. These providers process information according to their own policies and our instructions where applicable.

Cookies and analytics

The site may use cookies or similar technologies for basic functionality, security, or analytics. You can adjust browser settings to limit cookies, though some features may not work as expected.

Data retention

We retain inquiry and business records for as long as reasonably needed to respond, quote, deliver services, maintain records, and resolve disputes. You may request deletion of an inquiry by emailing edward@stoneindustriesusa.com with enough detail to identify the submission.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Please do not submit passwords, payment card numbers, bank account details, Social Security numbers, or medical records through website forms.

Your choices

You may contact us by email or phone instead of using online forms. California residents and other users may have rights to access, correct, or delete certain personal information under applicable law. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email above. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe as required by law.

Children's privacy

This site is intended for business and adult users. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
