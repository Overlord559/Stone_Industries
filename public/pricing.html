How it works Quote, scope, and payment

  1. You choose a starting package or describe what you need.
  2. Stone Industries confirms scope, timeline, and price in writing (email or quote/invoice).
  3. You receive a written quote or invoice — often sent through Bluevine invoices or payment links.
  4. For larger jobs, a deposit is required before work begins to reserve time and cover planning/setup.
  5. The final balance is due before launch, handoff, transfer, or final delivery unless we agree otherwise in writing.
  6. Extra work needs written approval and may be billed hourly or quoted separately.

No card data is collected on this website. There is no instant checkout here — payment happens after your quote is confirmed.

Deposits How payment works

  • Under $300: Full payment upfront or due at appointment/completion unless agreed otherwise.
  • $300–$999: Typically 50% deposit to schedule work; balance due before delivery or handoff.
  • $1,000+: Typically 40–50% deposit; balance due before launch, transfer, or final handoff.
  • Monthly care or support: Paid upfront monthly when offered.
  • Custom work: Quote first — no fixed price until scope is written.
  • Out-of-scope work: Often $85–$125/hr or a separate written quote.

Stone Industries may send payment requests through Bluevine invoices or payment links. Stripe Payment Links, invoices, or Checkout may be used later for specific packages — still not collected on this site.

Scope Scope protection

Starting prices apply only to the listed package scope. The final quote will spell out what is included.

  • Customer-requested changes, extra pages, extra workflows, extra devices, emergency support, additional on-site time, or third-party vendor delays may change the final price.
  • Any major scope change should be approved in writing (email is fine) before work continues.

Delivery Final delivery policy

  • Websites: A preview or review may be shared before final payment, but final launch, transfer, or handoff happens after the final balance is paid unless agreed otherwise in writing.
  • AI / workflow systems: Documentation, credentials, automations, or final handoff are provided after the final balance is paid.
  • Tech support: Payment is typically due at appointment or completion unless agreed otherwise.
  • PC builds / upgrades: Parts cost invoiced separately when Stone orders components; service fee per written quote. Handoff after final balance unless agreed otherwise.
  • Logistics / operations: Deliverables are released after invoice terms are met.

Review Customer approval and completion

  • You get a chance to review completed work within the agreed scope.
  • Minor corrections inside the agreed scope are included when reasonable.
  • New requests outside the agreed scope are billed separately.

We aim for clear expectations and fair communication — not aggressive legal language on a small-business site.

Questions Concerns, disputes, and refunds

Deposits reserve project time and cover initial planning and setup work. Refunds or credits depend on work already performed, third-party costs already incurred, and the written scope. We do not promise unlimited refunds on the website.

If something does not look right, please raise concerns in writing (email is fine) so there is a clear record. We will work in good faith to resolve issues within the agreed scope.

This page is practical business information, not legal advice. For contract-heavy jobs, consider attorney review before relying on custom agreement language.
