How it works Quotes and payment
  1. Choose a starting package or describe what you need.
  2. Stone Industries confirms scope, timeline, and price in writing (email or quote/invoice).
  3. You receive a written quote or invoice — often through Bluevine invoices or payment links.
  4. For larger jobs, a deposit is required before work begins.
  5. The final balance is due before launch, handoff, transfer, or final delivery unless agreed otherwise in writing.
  • Under $300: Paid upfront (Remote Quick Fix, Remote Business Tech Session).
  • $297–$750: Paid upfront · audit fee 100% credited toward sprint if sprint starts within 7 days when applicable.
  • $750–$1,500: 50% upfront / 50% before launch · or 40% start / 30% midpoint / 30% launch.
  • $1,500–$2,500: 50% upfront / 50% before launch · or 40% start / 30% midpoint / 30% before launch.
  • $2,500+: 30% deposit / 40% build approval / 30% before launch · usage/subscription costs separate.
  • Monthly (Managed AI Ops): First month upfront · recurring invoiced before each service period.
  • Custom work: Quote first — no fixed price until scope is written.

No card data is collected on this website. There is no instant checkout here — payment happens after your quote is confirmed. Stone Industries may send payment requests through Bluevine or Stripe Payment Links later for specific packages — still not collected on this site.

Scope Handoff, delivery, and changes

Starting prices apply only to the listed package scope. Extra pages, workflows, devices, on-site time, or third-party delays may change the final price. Major scope changes need written approval (email is fine) before work continues.

  • Websites: Preview may be shared before final payment; launch/transfer/handoff after final balance unless agreed otherwise.
  • AI / workflows: Documentation, credentials, and handoff after final balance.
  • Tech support: Payment typically due at appointment or completion unless agreed otherwise.
  • PC builds: Parts invoiced separately when Stone orders components; handoff after final balance.
  • Operations: Deliverables released after invoice terms are met.
  • Review: Minor corrections inside agreed scope are included when reasonable; new requests are billed separately.
